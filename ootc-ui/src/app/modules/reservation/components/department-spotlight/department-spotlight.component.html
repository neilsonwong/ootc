Kitchen

The Kitchen Team focuses on preparing, cooking, and serving 60+ meals to our guests during the dinner service. The team prepares a unique menu each week consisting of soup, main and dessert.

Tasks include: grocery shopping, food prep, cooking, dish-washing, and other kitchen duties as required

Note: Volunteers are especially needed for Monday morning to prepare breakfast for overnight guests.

IMPORTANT: The Kitchen Teamrecommended for highly experienced cooks and not beginners. If you sign up for one of these roles, we may contact you to follow up for more information.

Hospitality

The Hospitality Team focuses on providing a warm and friendly experience to guests. The team handles serving snacks and dinner, replenishing coffee and tea, and is highly encouraged to spend time with guests in conversation or activity.

Tasks include: serving snacks (3:30pm-6pm), serving dinner (6pm-9:30pm), socializing with guests, and other general duties required.

Clothing Bank

The Clothing Bank Team focuses on distributing various clothing items to our guests and organizing new apparel that is purchased or donated. The team aims to assist guest in finding what they need and ensuring that all guests are able to get what they what they need.

Tasks include: organizing and categorizing apparel, assisting guests in selecting apparel, and other duties as required.

Setup/Cleanup

The Setup/Cleanup Team focuses on setting up and cleaning up the rooms for dining and various services. The team handles setup Sunday afternoon before guests arrive and cleanup on Monday morning after guests depart.

Tasks include: setting up tables, chairs, and specialized layouts for service rooms.

Note: Volunteers are especially needed for Monday morning to cleanup the area.

Registration

The Registration Team focuses on attending to guests and volunteers entering and exiting the premises. The team checks-in guests and coordinates services for the guests such as showers, hair cuts, medical services and social services.

Tasks include: front desk administration, service scheduling and coordination, and other duties as required.

Note: Volunteers are especially needed for Monday morning during the departure of overnight guests.
